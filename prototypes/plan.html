Create Your Plan

Use "Inversion" to plan how you'll avoid procrastination triggers.

Your 'IF' should be the moment before a big decision. Think about when you typically procrastinate.

Your 'THEN' should be the action of identifying and avoiding failure triggers.

See examples
IF: "I sit down to write my report..."
THEN I WILL: "Ask myself: What would guarantee I don't finish? Then remove those distractions first."
IF: "I'm about to start my workout..."
THEN I WILL: "List what would make me skip it (phone notifications, uncomfortable clothes, no water), then prevent each one."
